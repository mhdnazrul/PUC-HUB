Spaced Repetition
Review material at increasing intervals — 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks. Dramatically improves long-term retention.
Active Recall
Close your book and try to recall key points from memory. Testing yourself is far more effective than passive re-reading.
Pomodoro Technique
Study 25 minutes, break 5 minutes. After 4 cycles, take a 20-minute break. Keeps focus sharp and prevents burnout.
Weak Areas First
Spend more time on subjects below 40%. Subjects above 80% only need light review before the exam day.
